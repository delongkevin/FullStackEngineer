How to Play:

  • Click on the circles as quickly as possible before they disappear
  • Each circle gives you points based on its size and your current combo
  • Smaller circles are worth more points but harder to click
  • Click circles consecutively to build your combo multiplier
  • The game gets faster as you level up!
